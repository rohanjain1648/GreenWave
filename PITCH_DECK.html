Max-Pressure: The Algorithm That Wins

How It Works

At each intersection, every cycle:

  1. Count the queues (upstream and downstream)
  2. Compute pressure = upstream – (0.5 × downstream)
  3. Give green to highest pressure direction
  4. Repeat every 5–10 seconds

Why it wins:

  • ✓ Reads live data (ignores ancient timer)
  • ✓ Adapts to demand (long queue → longer green)
  • ✓ Provably optimal (Varaiya, UC Berkeley, 2013)
  • ✓ No training needed (works immediately)
